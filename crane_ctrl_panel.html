CRANE CONTROL PANEL


Crane Control Panels are essential for the safe and efficient operation of cranes. They allow operators to manage the movements of the crane, including lifting, lowering, and traveling, from a centralized location. These panels ensure precise control and can be customized with advanced features for enhanced functionality.


    Types of Crane Control Panels:

Standard Control Panel – Basic controls for crane operation, typically with buttons, levers, or switches.


Digital Control Panel – Features digital displays and programmable settings for greater control and diagnostics.


Wireless Control Panel – Allows operators to control the crane remotely, enhancing flexibility and safety.


Pendant Control Panel – Attached to a cable or mounted on the crane, offering easy control for the operator in proximity.


    Application :
  • Manufacturing Plants
  • Warehouses
  • Construction Sites
  • Ports
  • Heavy Equipment Handling


    • Customizable with safety features like emergency stop buttons, overload protection, and anti-collision systems.
