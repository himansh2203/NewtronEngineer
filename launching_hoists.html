LAUNCHING HOISTS


Launching Hoists are specialized cranes used in construction and infrastructure projects to lift and position heavy loads, especially in the erection of bridges and other large structures. These hoists provide high lifting capacities and precise control for vertical and horizontal movement.


    Types of Launching Hoists:

Fixed Launching Hoist – Installed on a fixed structure, typically used for vertical lifting in bridge construction.


Movable Launching Hoist – Can be relocated along the construction site, offering flexibility for large projects.


Self-Erecting Launching Hoist – Designed for quick assembly and disassembly, ideal for temporary projects.


Rail-Mounted Launching Hoist – Mounted on rails for precise horizontal and vertical movements, commonly used in rail or bridge construction.


    Application :
  • Bridge Construction
  • Tunnel Construction
  • High-Rise Building Erection
  • Large Infrastructure Projects


    • Customizable for specific project requirements, including capacity, reach, and lifting speed.
