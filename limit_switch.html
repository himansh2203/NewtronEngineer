LIMIT SWITCH


Limit Switches are safety devices used in cranes and other machinery to prevent over-travel or damage by stopping the movement when a preset limit is reached. They are essential for controlling crane motion, ensuring safe operation, and protecting equipment from mechanical failure.


    Types of Limit Switches:

Mechanical Limit Switch – Uses a physical actuator to detect position and stop movement


Electrical Limit Switch – Triggers an electrical circuit to stop the crane when the limit is reached.


Rotary Limit Switch – Monitors rotational movement and stops the crane at predefined angles.


Magnetic Limit Switch – Uses magnetic fields to detect position without direct contact, reducing wear.


    Application :
  • Cranes and Hoists
  • Conveyor Systems
  • Elevators and Lifts
  • Industrial Automation
  • Heavy Equipment
