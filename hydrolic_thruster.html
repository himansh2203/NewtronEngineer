HYDRAULIC THRUSTER BRAKE


Hydraulic Thruster Brakes are used in cranes and other heavy machinery to provide reliable and efficient braking. They use hydraulic power to engage the brake pads, ensuring smooth and controlled stopping, even under heavy loads.


    Types of Hydraulic Thruster Brakes:

Direct-Acting Thruster Brake – Provides quick and responsive braking by directly applying pressure to the brake pads.


Indirect-Acting Thruster Brake – Uses a mechanical linkage to transfer hydraulic pressure to the brake pads, offering gradual braking.


Spring-Loaded Hydraulic Thruster Brake – Combines hydraulic pressure with spring force for added reliability in emergency braking situations.


    Application :
  • Cranes and Hoists
  • Elevators
  • Conveyor Systems
  • Material Handling Equipment
  • Industrial Machinery


    • Customizable based on load capacity, response time, and specific safety requirements.
