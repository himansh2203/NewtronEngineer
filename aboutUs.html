AN INSIGHT INTO "NEWTRON ENGINEERS"

WE WOULD LIKE TO INTRODUCE OURSELVES AS A PROFESSIONALLY MANAGED COMPANY ENGAGED IN THE BUSINESS OF MANUFACTURING CRANES, HOISTS, WINCHES, GANTRY, LAUNCHING HOISTS, AND JIB CRANES ALONG WITH THE RELATED SPARES.
MANUFACTURING STARTED IN THE YEAR 2006 IN THE INDUSTRIAL TOWN OF FARIDABAD, HARYANA (INDIA)
Under THE LEADERSHIP OF Mr. Pramod (BE-Electrical) and Mr. Sandeep Kumar (B.Tech.-Mechanical and MBA Operation), TODAY, WITH MORE THAN 1 DECADE OF EXPERTISE, WE HAVE OVER 250 + WORKING EQUIPMENT TO OUR NAME WITH OVER 100 + SATISFIED CLIENTS ACROSS INDIA.
OUR CRANES ARE DESIGNED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE INDIAN STANDARDS (IS), IS 807, IS3177, IS4177, AND THE INTERPLANT STEEL STANDARDS (IPSS).
We ALSO HAVE THE PLEASURE OF DESIGNING CRANES AS PER INTERNATIONAL STANDARDS LIKE DIN, FEM, IPSS, ISO, and CMAA.
WE ARE ISO 9001:2015, ISO 45001-2018, AND ISO1 4001:2015 CERTIFIED.
WE HAVE PRESENCE ACROSS ALL INDUSTRIAL VERTICALS, IT IS ROADS, RAILWAYS, METRO CONSTRUCTION, WATERPROJECTS, ANDINDUSTRIALSECTORS, ETC.
WE ARE A REGISTERED SUPPLIER TO MAJOR EPC, I.E., DMRC, L&T, HCC, SIMPLEX, IJM, PERSYS-SDN BHD, CONTINENTAL,ITD-CEM, DSC, JMC APCO, ETC.
OUR EQUIPMENT HAS BEEN INSPECTED AND ACCEPTED BY REPUTED CONSULTANTS LIKE PDIL, RITES, CEIL, MECON,WAPCOS,DNV,TUV, TCS,TPL, SGS, ETC.
MANUFACTURING UNITS ARE STRATEGICALLY LOCATED AT FARIDABAD,CATERING TO ALL PARTS OF INDIA.
AFTER-SALES SERVICES ARE PROVIDED NATION WIDE BY FACTORY TRAINED PERSONNEL WITH SPARES BACKUP
